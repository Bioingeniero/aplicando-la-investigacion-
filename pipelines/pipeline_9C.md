# Appendix C: Thermal Treatment (Carbonization) Profile
**Associated Pipeline:** 9.0 (Phase 1 - Lever 3)
**Scope:** Pyrolysis of binder without oxidation.

## 1. Atmosphere Control
*   **Gas:** Nitrogen (N₂) or Argon.
*   **Flow Rate:** 5 Volume Changes per hour (Furnace Volume).
*   **Oxygen Sensor:** Must read **< 50 ppm O₂** before heating begins.

## 2. The Heating Profile (120 Hour Cycle)
Standard cycle to reach 950°C-1100°C safely.

1.  **De-binding Zone (Ambient to 600°C):**
    *   *Rate:* **5°C/hour**. (Very slow to allow volatile outgassing without bloating).
    *   *Hold:* 2 hours at 600°C.
2.  **Densification Zone (600°C to $T_{target}$):**
    *   *Rate:* 20°C/hour.
    *   *Targets:* 800°C, 900°C, 1000°C, 1100°C.
3.  **Soak:**
    *   Hold at $T_{target}$ for **10 hours**.
4.  **Cooling:**
    *   Natural cooling under N₂ flow until T < 150°C. (Opening early = oxidation).

## 3. Post-Process Metric
*   **Open Porosity (ASTM C830):** Measured via boiling water method.

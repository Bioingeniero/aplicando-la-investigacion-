# Appendix 11C: The Structural Track (Pressing & Thermal)
**Presentation Section:** 2. Theoretical Framework & 3. Experimental Proposal
**Slide Mapping:** Slide 7 (Shaping/Carbonization) & Slides 12-13 (Variables)

## 1. Variable B: Pressing Pressure (The "Mechanical Seal")
*   **Concept:** Controlling Open Porosity via Density.
*   **Range:** 150 MPa $\rightarrow$ 350 MPa (Isostatic).
*   **Safety Protocol:** **"Slow Release" Cycle.**
    *   Decompression rate **< 2 MPa/sec** to prevent laminar cracking (spring-back).

## 2. Variable C: Carbonization Temperature (The "Thermal Seal")
*   **Concept:** Controlling Binder Coke Shrinkage & Crystallinity.
*   **Range:** 800°C $\rightarrow$ 1100°C.
*   **Safety Protocol:** **Inert Atmosphere Control.**
    *   Oxygen < 50 ppm (Nitrogen purge) during the 120-hour cycle to prevent oxidation.

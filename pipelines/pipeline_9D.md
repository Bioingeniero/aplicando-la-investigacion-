# Appendix D: VPI (Vacuum Pressure Impregnation) Protocol
**Associated Pipeline:** 9.0 (Phase 1 - Lever 4)
**Scope:** Deep sealing of porosity with Silanes or PFPE Oils.

## 1. Process Setpoints
*   **Tank A:** Resin/Oil Reservoir.
*   **Tank B:** Impregnation Chamber (Heated).

## 2. VPI Cycle Sequence
1.  **Pre-Heat:** Heat brushes to 110°C (Dry out moisture) -> Transfer to Tank B.
2.  **Dry Vacuum:** Pull **< 5 mbar** (absolute) for 30 minutes. (Evacuate air from pores).
3.  **Flooding:** Introduce Impregnant while maintaining vacuum.
4.  **Wet Soak:**
    *   Hold vacuum for 15 minutes (bubble release).
5.  **Pressure Injection:**
    *   Vent to atmosphere -> Apply **+5 bar N₂ pressure**.
    *   Hold for 60 minutes. (Force liquid into micropores).
6.  **Drain & Cure:**
    *   Drain liquid.
    *   *Silane Cure:* 150°C for 4 hours.
    *   *Oil:* No cure required, just drain and wipe.

## 3. Acceptance Criteria
*   **Weight Gain:** $> 2.5\%$ wt (indicates successful penetration).

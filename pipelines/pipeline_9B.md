# Appendix B: Isostatic Pressing Cycle Definition
**Associated Pipeline:** 9.0 (Phase 1 - Lever 2)
**Scope:** Defect-free compaction of Green Bodies.

## 1. Equipment Settings
*   **Machine:** EPSI Cold Isostatic Press (CIP).
*   **Fluid:** Water + Anti-corrosion additive.
*   **Mold:** Polyurethane tooling (Shore 90A).

## 2. The "Crack-Free" Pressure Cycle
To prevent lamination cracks due to rapid spring-back, the following curve is mandatory for all samples > 200 MPa.

1.  **Fill & Air Purge:** 0 to 5 MPa (Fast).
2.  **Ramp Up:** 5 MPa/sec to Target Pressure ($P_{target}$).
3.  **Dwell (Compaction):** Hold at $P_{target}$ for **120 seconds**.
    *   *Targets:* 150, 200, 250, 300, 350 MPa.
4.  **Decompression 1 (Slow Release):**
    *   From $P_{target}$ to 50 MPa.
    *   *Rate:* **max 2 MPa/sec**. (CRITICAL STAGE).
5.  **Decompression 2 (Final Vent):**
    *   From 50 MPa to 0.
    *   *Rate:* Fast vent allowed.

## 3. Quality Check (Green State)
*   Visual inspection for laminar cracks.
*   Green Density measurement (Geometric).

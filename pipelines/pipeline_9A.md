# Appendix A: Binder Characterization & Mixing Protocol
**Associated Pipeline:** 9.0 (Phase 1 - Lever 1)
**Scope:** Standardization of rheology for Phenolic, Pitch, and Furan binders.

## 1. Characterization Metrics (Pre-Mix)
Before mixing, all binder batches must be normalized to ensure consistent "Green Body" density.

*   **Viscosity:** Measured via Brookfield Viscometer @ mixing temperature.
    *   *Target:* 500 - 1500 cPs.
    *   *Adjustment:* Dilution with solvent (Ethanol/Furfural) or temperature adjustment.
*   **Solid Content (Non-Volatile):** IEC 60450 Method.
    *   *Target:* >60% wt.
*   **Coking Value (Conradson):** ASTM D2416.
    *   *Target:* >45% (Critical for Furan/Pitch to ensure structure survival).

## 2. Mixing Parameters (Sigma Blade Mixer)
*   **Temperature Setpoints:**
    *   *Phenolic:* 40°C (Avoid premature curing).
    *   *Pitch:* 120°C (Must be molten, above softening point of 110°C).
    *   *Furan:* 25°C (Room Temp, acid catalyst added dropwise).
*   **Time:** 45 minutes total (15 dry mix + 30 wet mix).
*   **Torque Limit:** Stop if Torque > 80 Nm (Prevent motor burnout).

## 3. Safety (HSE)
*   **Furan:** Acid catalyst (H₂SO₄) is corrosive. Full PPE required.
*   **Pitch:** PAH fumes (Polycyclic Aromatic Hydrocarbons). Active extraction required.

## 1. Variable D: Impregnation Strategy (The "Safety Net")
*   **Baseline:** None (As-Sintered).
*   **Challenger 1:** **Hydrophobic Silane**.
    *   *Mechanism:* Chemical rejection of water at the pore surface.
*   **Challenger 2:** **PFPE Lubricating Oil**.
    *   *Mechanism:* **Dual Function.** Repels water AND provides lubrication to prevent dry running.

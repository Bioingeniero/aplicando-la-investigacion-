# Appendix 10C: The Structural Track (Pressing & Thermal)
**Role:** Mechanical and Thermal closure of porosity.

## 1. Variable B: Pressing Pressure (Mechanical Seal)
*   **Hypothesis:** Increasing density reduces the *volume* available for water uptake.
*   **Range:** 150 MPa $\rightarrow$ 350 MPa.
*   **Safety Protocol:** **"Slow Release" Cycle.**
    *   Decompression rate **< 2 MPa/sec** to prevent laminar cracking (spring-back) in high-density parts.

## 2. Variable C: Carbonization Temperature (Thermal Seal)
*   **Hypothesis:** Higher temperatures increase crystallinity (ordering) and shrink the binder coke, closing micropores.
*   **Range:** 800°C $\rightarrow$ 1100°C.
*   **Safety Protocol:** **Inert Atmosphere Control.**
    *   Oxygen < 50 ppm (Nitrogen purge) to prevent oxidation of the surface during the 120-hour cycle.

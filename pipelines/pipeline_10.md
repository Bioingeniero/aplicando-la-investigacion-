## Slide 2: The Core Problem (Root Cause)
*   **Diagnosis:** Swelling is a **Thermodynamic Equilibrium** failure.
*   **Mechanism:** The current Binder/Porosity network absorbs humidity, leading to dimensional expansion ($Z_{\perp}$) and mechanical lock-up.
*   **Solution Strategy:** "Don't fix the symptom, close the pores."

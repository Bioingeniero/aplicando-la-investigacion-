# Appendix 11E: Validation Gates (The "Pass/Fail" Criteria)
**Presentation Section:** 3. Experimental Proposal (Outcomes)
**Slide Mapping:** Final Recommendations

## 1. Gate 1: Swelling Resistance (The Goal)
*   **Test:** **Accelerated Hydrothermal Aging (AHA)**.
*   **Condition:** Autoclave @ 120°C / 2 bar vapor / 48 Hours. (Simulates >2000h operational life).
*   **Metric:** **Anisotropic Swelling ($Z_{\perp}$)**.
*   **Target:** $< 0.1\%$ dimensional change perpendicular to pressing.

## 2. Gate 2: Tribological Safety (The Risk)
*   **Test:** Dynamic Run (Pin-on-Disc).
*   **Load:** 20 A/cm² (Overload).
*   **Metric:** Friction Coefficient ($\mu$).
*   **Target:** **$\mu < 0.25$**.
    *   *Failure Mode:* If $\mu > 0.25$, the formulation is too dry -> Mandatory switch to PFPE Oil Impregnation.
